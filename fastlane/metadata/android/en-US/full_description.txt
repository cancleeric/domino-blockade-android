Domino Blockade is a fast-paced domino game featuring the classic blockade ruleset.

Features:
• 2–4 player Pass & Play on a single device
• AI opponents with Easy, Medium, and Hard difficulty
• Game history, player statistics, and leaderboard
• High-contrast accessibility theme
• Adaptive layout for phones and tablets
• Dark mode support

How to play:
Match the pips on your tiles to the open ends of the board. When no player can place a tile and the boneyard is empty, the game is blocked. The player with the lowest pip count wins!

Domino Blockade is free to play and contains no ads.
